## 重点语句

### 谈论物品的数量和价格

```text
We want to go shopping today.

我们今天想去购物。
```

```text
Do you want to buy some clothes?

你想买些衣服吗？
```

```text
I want to by two dresses.

我想要买两件连衣裙。
```

```text
How much is this book?

这本书多少钱？
```

```text
That book is sixteen dollars.

那本书十六美元。
```
