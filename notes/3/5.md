# 第四部分

## 语法点

### 未来

> 要用应用锁**我**`打算`**学习**或者**你**`会`**学习**的话，就说  
> I'm going to study 或 You're going to study。

| 主语           | 动词（to eat）              |
| -------------- | --------------------------- |
| I              | am going to cook dinner.    |
| you / we/ they | are going to go home.       |
| he / she / it  | is going to leave tomorrow. |

## 重点语句

### 谈论日常安排

```text
What are we going to do tomorrow?

我们明天要做什么？
```

```text
Let's meet at a cafe tomorrow.

咱们明天在咖啡馆见面吧。
```

```text
What time do you want to meet?

你想要几点见？
```

```text
Can we meet at 11 a.m.?

我们可以上午十一点见吗？
```

```text
Please call me tonight.

请今晚给我打电话。
```
