# 终极对比表

| 类型    | 问什么？       | 例子                  | 错误示范                  |
| ------- | -------------- | --------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Do you  | 动作（动词）   | Do you eat breakfast? | ❌ Are you eat breakfast? |
| Are you | 状态/身份/地点 | Are you hungry?       | ❌ Do you hungry?         |
